Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Shellcove 隐私政策

生效日期 / Effective date: 2026-05-08

Architecture Overview / 架构概览

简体中文

Shellcove 不是 SaaS。它由两部分组成：iOS App（Shellcove）和你自托管的开源守护进程（shellcoved）。两者之间通过你信任的网络（局域网、Tailscale、Cloudflare Tunnel 等）直连，不经过任何 Shellcove 控制的服务器。

我们没有用户账号系统、没有数据库、没有日志服务器。我们看不到你的 prompt、代码、shell 输出、文件内容、IP 地址或任何其他使用数据 — 因为这些数据物理上从未离开你和你机器之间的加密连接。

English

Shellcove is not a SaaS. It is two pieces: an iOS app (Shellcove) and an open-source daemon (shellcoved) that you host yourself. The two communicate directly over a network you trust (local Wi-Fi, Tailscale, Cloudflare Tunnel, etc.), never through a server we control.

We have no user accounts, no databases, no log servers. We cannot see your prompts, code, shell output, file contents, IP addresses, or any other usage data — because that data physically never leaves the encrypted link between you and your machine.

Data Collection / 数据收集

简体中文

Shellcove iOS App 不收集、存储或传输任何用户个人信息到我们或任何第三方的服务器。使用本应用无需注册账号，我们不会收集您的姓名、电子邮件、位置信息、设备标识或任何其他个人身份信息。

本地存储的内容仅包括：配对密钥（X25519 私钥）、可信主机指纹、保存的 SSH 凭据（如开启了 Legacy SSH Mode）、以及你的 App 设置。这些数据保存在 iOS Keychain 和 UserDefaults 中，受 iOS 系统级保护。

English

The Shellcove iOS app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers or any third party. No account is required. We do not collect your name, email address, location, device identifier, or any other personally identifiable information.

What is stored on-device only: pairing keys (X25519 private keys), trusted-host fingerprints, saved SSH credentials (only if Legacy SSH Mode is enabled), and your app settings. These live in the iOS Keychain and UserDefaults under iOS system-level protection.

Encryption / 加密

简体中文
  • 配对：X25519 ECDH 密钥协商，daemon 永远不知道 App 的私钥，反之亦然
  • 会话流量：每一帧 WebSocket 都用 AES-256-GCM 用配对密钥加封
  • SSH（Legacy 模式）：使用 Apple SwiftNIO SSH，凭据存于 iOS Keychain
  • 合规：仅使用标准 SSH/TLS 与 Apple CryptoKit，按 §740.17(b) of the EAR 属于豁免出口管制类别。Info.plist 中 ITSAppUsesNonExemptEncryption = false
English
  • Pairing: X25519 ECDH key agreement; the daemon never learns the app's private key and vice versa
  • Session traffic: every WebSocket frame is sealed with AES-256-GCM under the paired key
  • SSH (Legacy mode): Apple SwiftNIO SSH; credentials live in the iOS Keychain
  • Compliance: only standard SSH/TLS and Apple CryptoKit are used. Exempt under §740.17(b) of the EAR. ITSAppUsesNonExemptEncryption = false in Info.plist.

Network Activity / 网络活动

简体中文

本应用产生的网络流量只有一种 — 与你已配对的 shellcoved 守护进程的直连。没有 telemetry，没有 crash reporting 上报，没有 analytics，没有第三方 SDK 网络请求，没有广告网络。

iOS 在首次连接局域网设备时会请求 Local Network 权限；扫描配对二维码时会请求摄像头权限。两者都仅用于其文档化的用途，且可在系统设置中随时撤销。

English

The only network traffic the app generates is the direct connection to a shellcoved daemon you have paired with. No telemetry, no crash reporting, no analytics, no third-party SDK network calls, no ad networks.

iOS requests Local Network permission the first time you connect to a daemon on your LAN, and Camera permission when you scan a pairing QR code. Both are used strictly for their documented purpose and can be revoked from iOS Settings at any time.

Permissions / 权限说明

简体中文
  • 摄像头：仅在 PairingView 用于扫描配对二维码，从不在其他时刻激活
  • 本地网络：用于发现并连接同一网络下的 shellcoved 守护进程（Bonjour _shellcoved._tcp）
  • 通知（本地）：用于在锁屏上推送 agent 权限请求与连接事件，无远程推送服务，App 不持有 Push Notifications entitlement
  • Face ID / Touch ID：用于在锁屏直接 Allow / Deny 时确认用户身份
English
  • Camera: active only on the PairingView to scan the pairing QR code; never accessed elsewhere
  • Local Network: used to discover and connect to a shellcoved daemon on your network (Bonjour _shellcoved._tcp)
  • Notifications (local only): used to surface agent permission requests and connection events on the lock screen. No remote push, no Push Notifications entitlement.
  • Face ID / Touch ID: confirms user identity for lock-screen Allow / Deny responses

Third Parties / 第三方

简体中文

Shellcove iOS App 不内嵌任何第三方分析、广告网络、归因 SDK、崩溃上报或追踪 SDK。我们不会向任何第三方出售、出租或分享您的数据 — 因为我们没有任何您的数据可分享。

shellcoved 守护进程在你机器上启动 AI 编码代理 CLI（如 Claude Code、Codex 等）时，这些 CLI 会按各自的隐私政策与对应厂商的服务通信。请参阅相应 CLI 厂商的隐私政策。

English

The Shellcove iOS app embeds no third-party analytics, ad networks, attribution SDKs, crash reporters, or tracking SDKs. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party — because we have no data of yours to share.

When the shellcoved daemon launches an AI coding agent CLI (Claude Code, Codex, etc.) on your machine, that CLI communicates with its own vendor's services per its own privacy policy. Please consult the privacy policy of the CLI vendor in question.

Children / 儿童

简体中文

Shellcove 是一款面向开发者的工具产品，不针对 13 岁以下儿童。我们也不收集任何用户数据，因此不存在儿童信息处理的情形。

English

Shellcove is a developer tool and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We collect no user data, so no children's data is ever processed.
