走起 ZouQi · 出门规划师

别纠结去哪，走起就对了

走起把「附近哪里真好吃、这个季节怎么玩」变成一条能马上出发的路线。不做评分榜，只给一句话理由 + 一条顺路的安排；想去就出发，整条路线一键接力到高德 / 百度 / Apple 地图导航，也能单独导航某一个点。首版聚焦上海「梧桐区」（衡复风貌区，徐汇 / 黄浦交界）。

附近吃喝玩乐 路线方案，不是地点列表 一句话真实适配理由 整条路线一键导航 高德 / 百度 / Apple 地图 无账号 · 无广告 不收集数据
即将上线 App Store · Coming soon iOS 17+ · 简体中文 / English
English

Stop deciding. Just go.

ZouQi turns "where's actually good to eat nearby, and what's fun this season" into a route you can set out on right now. No rating leaderboards — just one honest line on why a spot fits, and a walkable plan that flows. Hand the whole route to Amap, Baidu Maps or Apple Maps in one tap, or navigate a single stop. The first release focuses on Shanghai's plane-tree neighborhood (Hengfu / Xuhui–Huangpu).

Routes, not lists / 路线，不是清单
简体中文
  • 把零散的点连成一条线：傍晚去江边、深夜觅食、梧桐漫步、就近吃饭——按场景给现成的路线
  • 每个去处只有一句话：为什么值得、什么时候去最好，不堆评分和点评
  • 远途自驾攻略（如北京→新疆）按天分段，充电 / 住 / 看点 / 提示一目了然
English
  • Scattered spots strung into one line: riverside at dusk, late-night bites, plane-tree strolls, food close by — ready-made routes by mood
  • One line per spot: why it's worth it and when it's best — no rating piles
  • Long road-trip guides (e.g. Beijing → Xinjiang) split by day, with charging / stay / sights / tips
One-tap navigation / 一键导航
简体中文
  • 整条路线按顺序一次性接进地图 App：高德、百度都支持途经点，Apple 地图也能起步
  • 也能只导航单个点位；坐标按目标地图自动换算（高德 GCJ-02、百度 BD-09）不偏移
  • 未装某个地图时自动回退到 Apple 地图，不卡住
English
  • Hand the whole route, in order, to a maps app — Amap and Baidu support waypoints; Apple Maps works too
  • Or navigate a single stop; coordinates are auto-converted per target map (GCJ-02 for Amap, BD-09 for Baidu) so pins land right
  • Falls back to Apple Maps when a chosen app isn't installed
Built for the neighborhood / 为本地而做
简体中文
  • 「离你最近」：按你的定位把顺路的好去处排在前面
  • 清真认证标注：吃得放心，专门的信任标识
  • 知道一个别人不知道的好地方？在地图上直接选点推荐给大家
English
  • "Closest to you": spots on your way float to the top using your location
  • Halal-certified labels for eating with peace of mind
  • Know a good spot others don't? Drop a pin right on the map to suggest it
Privacy / 隐私
简体中文
  • 完全离线运行：没有账号、没有服务器、没有后端，不联网上传任何数据
  • 定位只在本机用于「离你最近」排序和地图显示，绝不上传
  • 无第三方分析、广告、追踪 SDK。收藏只存在你自己的设备上
English
  • Fully offline: no account, no server, no backend — nothing is uploaded
  • Location is used on-device only, for "closest to you" sorting and the map — never uploaded
  • No third-party analytics, ads, or tracking SDKs. Favorites stay on your device
Requirements / 系统要求
简体中文
  • iOS / iPadOS 17.0 或更新版本
  • 界面支持简体中文与 English，可在设置内切换（默认跟随系统）
  • 导航接力需自行安装高德 / 百度地图；Apple 地图为系统自带
English
  • iOS / iPadOS 17.0 or newer
  • UI in Simplified Chinese and English, switchable in Settings (follows the system by default)
  • Route hand-off needs Amap / Baidu Maps installed; Apple Maps is built in
