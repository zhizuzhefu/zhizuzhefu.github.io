Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

走起 ZouQi 隐私政策

生效日期 / Effective date: 2026-06-03

Summary / 摘要

简体中文

走起 ZouQi 没有账号系统、不含任何第三方分析或广告 SDK、不做用户追踪。它只与「我们自己的」服务器通信，且仅限两件事：(1) 拉取只读的地点/路线内容（不发送你的任何信息）；(2) 当你主动「推荐一个玩法」时，提交你填写的地点信息。你的设备定位只在本机用于「离你最近」排序和地图显示，绝不上传。除此之外我们不收集任何可识别你身份的信息。

English

ZouQi has no account system, no third-party analytics or advertising SDKs, and does no user tracking. It talks only to our own server, and only for two things: (1) fetching read-only place/route content (sending none of your information); (2) submitting a spot when you choose to "suggest a spot." Your device location is used on-device only — for "closest to you" sorting and the map — and is never uploaded. Beyond that we collect nothing that identifies you.

Data Collection / 数据收集

简体中文

走起不要求注册或登录，不收集你的姓名、邮箱、电话或任何账号身份信息。app 的全部核心浏览功能在断网时也能用（回退到内置内容）。唯二会离开设备的数据是：拉取内容的网络请求，以及你主动提交的投稿（见下）。与任何网络请求一样，我们的 CDN（Cloudflare）会看到你的 IP；我们仅将其哈希后短期用于防滥用频控，不用它识别你或做画像。

English

ZouQi requires no registration or sign-in and collects no name, email, phone, or account identity. All core browsing works offline (falling back to built-in content). The only data that leaves your device is the content-fetch request and any spot you choose to submit (below). As with any network request, our CDN (Cloudflare) sees your IP; we only hash it for short-term abuse rate-limiting, never to identify or profile you.

Location / 位置信息

简体中文

走起请求「使用 App 期间」的定位权限，仅用于两件事：(1) 在地图上显示你的位置；(2) 把「离你最近」的好去处排在前面。位置数据只在你的设备上处理，不会上传到任何服务器，也不会与任何第三方共享。你可以拒绝授权，应用其余功能不受影响（只是不再按距离排序）。

English

ZouQi requests "While Using the App" location access for two purposes only: (1) showing your position on the map; (2) sorting nearby spots by distance. Location data is processed only on your device and is never uploaded to any server or shared with any third party. You may decline — the rest of the app keeps working (it just stops sorting by distance).

Opening Map Apps / 唤起地图 App

简体中文

当你点击「导航」时，走起会把你选择的目的地名称与坐标交给你选定的地图 App（Apple 地图、高德地图或百度地图）来规划路线。这一步会离开走起、进入对应地图 App，之后的数据处理适用该地图 App 自己的隐私政策。走起本身不向这些服务发送除「你正要导航的目的地」之外的任何信息，也不会发送你的身份。

English

When you tap "Navigate," ZouQi passes the chosen destination's name and coordinates to the maps app you select (Apple Maps, Amap, or Baidu Maps) to plan the route. This leaves ZouQi and enters that maps app, whose own privacy policy then governs the handling of data. ZouQi sends nothing beyond the destination you are navigating to, and does not send your identity.

Content Updates / 内容更新

简体中文

为了让地点、路线和攻略不发版也能更新，走起会定期向我们自己的只读内容接口请求最新数据。这个请求不携带你的任何信息（除上面提到的、任何联网都会有的 IP）。拉取失败时回退到上次缓存或 app 内置数据，因此断网/接口不可用也能正常浏览。

English

So places, routes and guides can update without an app release, ZouQi periodically requests the latest data from our own read-only content endpoint. This request carries none of your information (beyond the IP inherent to any network call, as noted above). If it fails, the app falls back to the last cache or the built-in content, so browsing keeps working offline.

Spot Submissions / 推荐投稿

简体中文

只有当你主动使用「推荐一个玩法」并点击提交时，走起才会把你填写的内容发送到我们的服务器：地点名称、你在地图上选取的坐标、一句话理由，以及 app 版本号。投稿是匿名的——不绑定账号、不含你的身份。提交的内容进入人工审核队列，通过后才可能出现在 app 里。这些内容仅用于审核与丰富地点库。

English

Only when you actively use "Suggest a spot" and tap submit does ZouQi send what you entered to our server: the spot name, the coordinate you picked on the map, your one-line reason, and the app version. Submissions are anonymous — no account, no identity attached. They enter a manual review queue and may appear in the app only after approval. This content is used solely to review and enrich the place library.

Local Storage / 本地存储

简体中文

走起只在本机的系统 UserDefaults 里保存少量你的偏好：收藏的去处 / 攻略、语言设置。这些数据只存在你的设备上，删除应用即一并清除。你在「推荐一个玩法」里于地图上选取的位置，仅用于本次填写，不会被传输到任何服务器。

English

ZouQi stores only a small amount of your preferences in the device's standard UserDefaults: your saved spots / guides and your language choice. This stays on your device and is removed when you delete the app. A location you pick on the map in "Suggest a spot" is used only for that entry and is not transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy / 儿童隐私

简体中文

走起不面向 13 岁以下儿童，不会有意收集儿童数据。鉴于本应用不收集任何用户数据，对各年龄段用户均无数据相关风险。

English

ZouQi is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children. Since the app collects no user data at all, this poses no data-related risk to any age group.

Changes / 政策变更

简体中文

如果本隐私政策有变更，我们会更新本页顶部的「生效日期」。若涉及数据处理方式的实质改变，新版本发布时会在更新说明中明确告知。

English

If this policy changes, we update the "Effective date" at the top of this page. Substantive changes to how data is handled will be highlighted in the corresponding app release notes.
