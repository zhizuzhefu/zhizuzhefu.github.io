Navigation & map apps / 导航与地图 App

简体中文
  • 整条路线导航：在某条路线或攻略详情页点「导航整条路线」，选地图 App，所有途经点会按顺序接进去。高德、百度支持完整途经点；Apple 地图会显示全部点位、按首尾规划路线。
  • 单点导航：在某个地点详情页点「带我去这儿」即可只导航这一个点。
  • 选项里没有高德 / 百度？需要先在 App Store 安装对应地图；未安装时走起会自动用 Apple 地图。
  • 点位有偏移？走起会按目标地图自动换算坐标系（高德 GCJ-02、百度 BD-09）。若仍有偏差，多为该地点坐标本身待校准，欢迎邮件反馈具体地点。
English
  • Whole-route navigation: on a route or guide detail page, tap "Navigate full route" and pick a maps app — every stop is handed over in order. Amap and Baidu support full waypoints; Apple Maps shows all pins and routes first-to-last.
  • Single stop: on a spot's detail page, tap "Take me there" to navigate just that point.
  • No Amap / Baidu in the list? Install the maps app from the App Store first; if it's missing, ZouQi falls back to Apple Maps.
  • Pin slightly off? ZouQi auto-converts coordinates per target map (GCJ-02 for Amap, BD-09 for Baidu). If it's still off, the spot's own coordinate likely needs calibration — email us the spot.

Location permission / 定位权限

简体中文
  • 走起只在你点定位或进入地图时请求「使用 App 期间」的定位，用于地图显示和「离你最近」排序。
  • 位置只在本机使用，不上传。详见 隐私政策。
  • 不想授权也能用：其余功能正常，只是不再按距离排序。要改授权到 iOS 设置 → 走起 ZouQi → 位置。
English
  • ZouQi requests "While Using the App" location only when you tap locate or open the map — for the map and "closest to you" sorting.
  • Location stays on-device and is never uploaded. See the Privacy Policy.
  • You can decline and still use everything; it just stops sorting by distance. Change it in iOS Settings → ZouQi → Location.

Language / 语言

简体中文

走起界面支持简体中文与 English。默认跟随系统语言，也可在「设置 → 语言」里手动切换，切换后立即生效，无需重启。

English

ZouQi's interface supports Simplified Chinese and English. It follows the system language by default, and you can switch manually in Settings → Language — it takes effect immediately, no restart needed.

Suggest a spot / 推荐一个玩法

简体中文

知道一个好地方？在「推荐一个玩法」里给个名字、在地图上拖动把图钉对准位置、点「选这里」、写一句为什么值得就行。提交后会匿名发送到我们的服务器进入人工审核，通过后才会出现在 app 里——不绑定账号、不含你的身份。详见隐私政策。

English

Know a good place? In "Suggest a spot," give it a name, drag the map to aim the pin, tap "Choose this spot," and add one line on why it's worth it. On submit it's sent anonymously to our server for manual review and appears in the app only after approval — no account, no identity attached. See the Privacy Policy.

Coverage / 覆盖范围

简体中文

首版内容聚焦上海「梧桐区」（衡复历史风貌区，徐汇 / 黄浦交界）的吃喝玩乐与散步路线，外加少量远途自驾攻略示例。后续版本会按真实反馈扩展更多区域。

English

The first release focuses on Shanghai's plane-tree neighborhood (the Hengfu historic area, Xuhui–Huangpu border) — food, fun, and walking routes — plus a few long road-trip guide samples. More areas will follow based on real feedback.
