FAQ / 常见问题

简体中文
  • 调色或表情会改原图吗？不会。所有编辑都是无损的，原图始终保留，导出才生成新文件。
  • 需要联网吗？不需要。LiveLayout 完全在设备本地运行。
  • 导出的实况照片不动？请在相册中长按播放；导出为 Live Photo 后即可看到动画。
  • 怎么切换中英文？在 App 内「关于 → 语言」中切换，或跟随系统。
English
  • Does editing change my original? No. All edits are non-destructive; the original is kept and a new file is created only on export.
  • Do I need internet? No. LiveLayout runs entirely on-device.
  • My exported Live Photo doesn't move? Press and hold it in Photos to play; exporting as a Live Photo enables the animation.
  • How do I switch language? In the app under About → Language, or follow the system.
