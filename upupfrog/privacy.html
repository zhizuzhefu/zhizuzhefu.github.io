Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

涨涨蛙隐私政策

生效日期 / Effective date: 2026-04-21

Data Collection / 数据收集

简体中文

涨涨蛙不收集、存储或传输任何用户个人信息。使用本应用无需注册账号或登录，我们不会收集您的姓名、电子邮件、位置信息或任何其他个人身份信息。

您设置的薪资、工作时间等数据仅保存在您的设备本地（UserDefaults），不会上传至任何服务器。

English

upupfrog does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. No account registration or sign-in is required. We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any other personally identifiable information.

Your salary, work schedule, and other settings are stored locally on your device (UserDefaults) and are never uploaded to any server.

Network Usage / 网络使用

简体中文

涨涨蛙的唯一网络请求是获取实时汇率数据，用于多币种换算功能。此请求仅包含货币代码，不涉及任何用户身份信息、薪资数据或设备标识。

如果网络不可用，应用将使用内置的离线汇率作为备用，所有核心功能不受影响。

English

The only network request upupfrog makes is to fetch live exchange rate data for multi-currency conversion. This request contains only currency codes — no user identity, salary data, or device identifiers are transmitted.

If the network is unavailable, the app falls back to built-in offline exchange rates. All core features work without an internet connection.

Permissions / 权限说明

简体中文
  • 通知（可选）：用于发送每日上班提醒和下班收入播报。此权限为可选项，拒绝授权不影响任何核心功能。
  • App Group：用于主应用与桌面小组件之间同步设置数据，数据仅在本设备内传递。
English
  • Notifications (optional): Used to deliver daily work-start reminders and end-of-day earnings summaries. This permission is optional — declining it does not affect any core functionality.
  • App Group: Used to sync settings between the main app and the Home Screen widget. Data is only shared within your device.

Data Sharing & Third Parties / 数据共享与第三方

简体中文

涨涨蛙不会出售、出租或以任何方式向第三方提供您的个人数据。本应用不包含第三方分析工具、广告 SDK 或跨应用追踪功能。由于本应用不收集用户数据，因此不存在与第三方共享数据的情形。

English

upupfrog does not sell, rent, or otherwise share your personal data with any third party. The app does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking. Since the app does not collect user data, there is no data to share with third parties.

Policy Updates / 政策更新

简体中文

本隐私政策可能会不定期更新。如有重大变更，我们将在本页面发布修订版本并更新生效日期。建议您定期查阅本页面以了解最新的隐私保护措施。

English

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. If significant changes are made, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information on our privacy practices.
