  • 8 个内置预设：奶茶、外卖、电影票、iPhone、MacBook 等常见消费
  • 15 种货币支持：自动汇率换算，出差旅行也能用
  • 桌面小组件：不打开 App 也能看到今日收入
English
  • Real-time earnings counter: watch your salary tick up every second
  • Work-time converter: enter a price, see how long you'd need to work for it
  • 8 built-in presets: milk tea, takeout, movie tickets, iPhone, MacBook, and more
  • 15 currencies supported with live exchange rates
  • Home Screen widget: check today's earnings at a glance

Privacy / 隐私保护
简体中文

涨涨蛙不收集任何个人信息。你的薪资数据仅存储在设备本地，不会上传到任何服务器。

唯一的网络请求是获取汇率数据，用于多币种换算，不涉及任何用户身份或薪资信息。

English

upupfrog does not collect any personal information. Your salary data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

The only network request fetches exchange rate data for multi-currency conversion — no user identity or salary information is involved.
