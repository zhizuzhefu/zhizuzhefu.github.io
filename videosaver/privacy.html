影存隐私政策

最近更新 / Last updated: 2026-06-24

简体中文

一句话说明

影存是一款本地优先的素材保存与管理工具。你保存的素材、标签和编辑信息默认保存在设备本地；我们不收集个人信息，不出售数据，不使用广告、分析或跨 App 追踪 SDK。

我们处理什么

  • 你主动粘贴或分享给 App 的 URL、文本、图片或视频。
  • 保存素材所需的文件名、来源域名、时长、大小、标签、裁切比例和缩略图。
  • 你主动通过邮件或反馈页面提交的内容。

网络访问

当你提交链接时，影存会访问该公开链接以解析可保存资源；也可能访问公开 JSON 配置文件以更新平台解析规则。影存不会读取其他 App 的私有数据，不会帮助绕过登录权限、DRM、付费墙、私密内容或平台限制。

本地存储

素材文件、标签、编辑元数据和下载记录默认保存在你的设备本地。卸载 App 或清理 App 数据可能会删除这些本地文件。

数据共享

我们不会出售、出租或共享你的个人素材。除非你主动导出、分享或提交反馈，影存不会向第三方发送你的素材内容。

联系我们

隐私相关问题请通过 zhizuzhefu@foxmail.com 联系我们。

English

Summary

YingCun is a local-first media saver and organizer. Saved assets, tags, and edit metadata stay on your device by default. We do not collect personal information, sell data, or use advertising, analytics, or cross-app tracking SDKs.

What the app processes

  • URLs, text, images, or videos you paste or share into the app.
  • Metadata needed to save and organize assets, such as filename, source domain, duration, size, tags, crop-ratio notes, and thumbnails.
  • Feedback you intentionally send by email or through the feedback page.

Network access

When you submit a link, YingCun may access that public link to resolve saveable resources. It may also fetch public JSON config to update platform parsing rules. YingCun does not read private data from other apps and does not bypass login walls, DRM, paywalls, private content, or platform restrictions.

Local storage

Asset files, tags, edit metadata, and download records are stored locally on your device by default. Removing the app or clearing app data may delete these local files.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal assets. YingCun does not send your saved media to third parties unless you explicitly export, share, or submit feedback.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact zhizuzhefu@foxmail.com.
