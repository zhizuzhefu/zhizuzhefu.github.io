Companion / 陪伴角色
简体中文

宽宽是一只小小的陪伴猫，会呼吸、会眨眼、会把信轻轻递给你。它不评判、不催促，只是说一句：我在这里。

动效强度可在设置中调到「柔和」或「关闭」，让你按自己的状态决定它有多活跃。

English

宽宽 ("Kuan Kuan") is a small round cat who breathes, blinks, and quietly hands you a letter. No judgment, no rush — just a small voice saying: I'm here.

Motion intensity is adjustable in Settings — standard, soft, or off, so you can choose how present they should be.

Tone Guide / 文案原则
简体中文
  • 不说教、不强迫积极
  • 不否定痛苦、不制造羞耻感
  • 不替你做人生判断、不承诺一定会变好
  • 不医疗化表达

每一条文案都经过严格审核，禁止「想开点」「别难过了」「比你惨的人多了」这类话出现。

English
  • Never preachy, never "stay positive"
  • Never dismisses your pain, never shames you
  • Never makes life decisions for you, never promises it'll get better
  • Never medicalizes your feelings

Every line is reviewed against this guide. Lines like "cheer up" or "others have it worse" are explicitly forbidden.
